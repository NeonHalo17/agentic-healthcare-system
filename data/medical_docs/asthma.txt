Disease: Asthma

Overview:
Asthma is a chronic inflammatory disease of the airways causing reversible airflow obstruction.

Symptoms:
- Wheezing
- Persistent cough
- Chest tightness
- Shortness of breath
- Night-time coughing

Risk Factors:
- Allergies
- Family history
- Air pollution
- Smoking
- Respiratory infections

Diagnosis:
- Spirometry
- Peak flow measurement
- Bronchodilator response test

Treatment:
- Inhaled corticosteroids
- Short-acting bronchodilators
- Long-acting bronchodilators
- Leukotriene receptor antagonists

Lifestyle Recommendations:
- Avoid allergens
- Carry rescue inhaler
- Follow asthma action plan
- Regular medical follow-up
