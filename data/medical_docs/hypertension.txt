Disease: Hypertension

Overview:
Hypertension is persistently elevated blood pressure that increases the risk of cardiovascular disease.

Symptoms:
- Often no symptoms
- Headaches
- Dizziness
- Blurred vision
- Chest pain (severe cases)
- Shortness of breath

Risk Factors:
- High salt diet
- Obesity
- Smoking
- Alcohol consumption
- Family history

Diagnosis:
- Blood pressure measurement
- Ambulatory blood pressure monitoring

Treatment:
- ACE inhibitors
- Calcium channel blockers
- Diuretics
- Beta blockers

Lifestyle Recommendations:
- Reduce salt intake
- Exercise regularly
- Lose excess weight
- Stop smoking
